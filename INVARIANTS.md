# Weaver OS Constitutional Invariants

Status: PRE-PRODUCTION / DOCTRINE BASELINE

These invariants constrain repository claims, runtime evolution, documentation, and release governance. They are not evidence of implementation by themselves.

## Master Invariant

No mechanism may silently convert uncertainty into authority.

## Core Authority Invariants

1. **COGNITION_IS_NOT_AUTHORITY** — Models, agents, simulations, forecasts, and generated plans may propose. They may not authorize themselves.
2. **PROPOSAL_IS_NOT_EXECUTION** — A proposal must pass the authority path before it changes trusted state.
3. **COORDINATION_IS_NOT_AUTHORIZATION** — Agreement among multiple agents, models, committees, or swarms does not grant executive power.
4. **FUNDER_IS_NOT_WITNESS** — A financial sponsor, beneficiary, or economically captured actor cannot serve as the independent witness for a value or validity claim.
5. **POSITIVE_OUTCOME_REQUIRES_ATTESTATION** — Claimed success, benefit, impact, or value requires a replayable PASS attestation before promotion.
6. **SIGNAL_IS_NOT_AUTHORITY** — Frontier signals, benchmarks, papers, demos, or market claims are intake events, not authority events.
7. **EXPLANATION_IS_NOT_EXECUTION** — Dashboards, visualizations, research papers, mythic documents, and interpretive layers are Authority Weight 0 unless explicitly wired through a verified gate.
8. **MYTHIC_CONTENT_IS_QUARANTINED** — Symbolic, esoteric, poetic, or mythological artifacts may be archived but may not define runtime semantics.
9. **REPLAY_SUPERSEDES_NARRATION** — Claims about behavior must yield to receipts and replay results.
10. **NO_PRODUCTION_TRUST_WITHOUT_INDEPENDENT_REPLAY** — L4 independent replay and L5 production observation are required before production readiness claims.

## Evidence Discipline

Existence is not execution. Execution is not verification. Verification is not independent replay. Independent replay is not production trust.

## Operator Compression

The group's whisper is not a command.
The patron's signature is not a witness.
The happy outcome is not yet true until the receipt is independently replayed.
