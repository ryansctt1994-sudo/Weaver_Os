# SIS Steward

SIS Steward is the accountability layer for the Weaver Governance Runtime.

It answers:

> Who owns the consequences of a decision?

Stewardship requirements:

- every authority claim must have an accountability owner,
- every promotion must have a review owner,
- every failure must have a repair path,
- every deployment claim must identify responsible stewards.

Status: `ACCOUNTABILITY_LAYER_EMERGING / NOT_AUTHORITY`
