# Limitations and Nonclaims

## Nonclaims

This package does not claim:

- independent replay
- independent replication
- adversarial reproduction
- external audit
- operational authority
- production authority

## Limitations

- Evidence is primarily local.
- Replay semantics are not fully mature.
- Witness independence is not fully established.
- Spec-to-code traceability is incomplete.
- Hardware evidence remains simulation-bound.
- External validation has not been earned.

## Scope limit

Reviewers are being asked to evaluate the package only against the claims and evidence declared here.

They are not being asked to validate the broader worldview, mythos, or future roadmap.

## Conclusion

These limitations are the necessary boundary conditions for honest review.
