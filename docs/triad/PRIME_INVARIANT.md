# Prime Invariant

No mechanism may silently convert uncertainty into authority.

This invariant is the reconstruction root for the Triad system. It applies to every authority claim, replay result, witness annotation, receipt, policy transition, and execution decision.

## Interpretation

Uncertainty may trigger investigation, simulation, warning, refusal, downgrade, or narrower authority.

Uncertainty may not silently create broader permission, higher trust, permanent legitimacy, or execution authority.

## Required Consequences

- Generation is not authority.
- Observation is not authority.
- Attestation is not authority.
- Narrative is not authority.
- Replay under current policy is required before recorded history becomes authoritative.
- When admissibility cannot be established, the system fails closed.

## Non-Claim

This invariant does not prove safety. It defines a rejection condition: any design that allows uncertainty to silently become authority violates the constitutional core of Weaver / Triad.
