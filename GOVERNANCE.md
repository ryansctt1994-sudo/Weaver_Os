## Governance Principle

No mechanism may silently convert uncertainty, unfamiliarity, consensus, continuity, or capability into authority.

## Authority Rule

Authority claims require:

1. admissible claim state,
2. grounded evidence,
3. preserved lineage,
4. replayable receipt,
5. promotion review,
6. accountability owner,
7. independent reproduction for higher evidence levels.

## Amendment Rule

No amendment is accepted as authoritative merely because it is merged.

An amendment becomes governance-relevant only when it has:

- source diff,
- review trail,
- receipt or rationale,
- authority impact statement,
- rollback path,
- steward owner.

## Promotion Rule

Promotion is separate from replay.

Replay may establish reconstructability. It does not establish authority.
